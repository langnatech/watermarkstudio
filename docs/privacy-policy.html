1. Information We Collect / 我们收集哪些信息

English
  • Media you select: To add or remove watermarks, the app accesses photos and videos you choose via the system photo picker. Processing is performed locally on your device. We do not upload your media to our servers.
  • On-device preferences: Such as subscription status and daily free export counts, stored in private app storage (SharedPreferences) on your device.
  • Subscriptions & purchases: If you subscribe to Pro, payments are handled by Google Play Billing. We receive subscription validity status from Google; we do not collect your card numbers.
  • Advertising data: The free tier displays Google AdMob ads. Google may collect advertising IDs, device information, IP address, and similar data per its policies. See Google Privacy Policy and AdMob help.

We do not require user accounts and do not collect names, emails, or phone numbers unless you contact us voluntarily.

中文
  • 您主动选择的媒体文件：为添加或去除水印，本应用会访问您通过系统相册/文件选择器选中的图片或视频。处理在设备本地完成，我们不会将照片或视频上传至自有服务器。
  • 设备本地设置：例如订阅状态、每日免费导出次数等，保存在设备上的应用私有存储（SharedPreferences）中。
  • 订阅与购买信息：若您购买 Pro 订阅，交易由 Google Play 结算处理。我们仅通过 Google Play Billing 接收订阅是否有效等信息，不直接收集银行卡号。
  • 广告相关数据：免费版展示 Google AdMob 广告。Google 可能根据其政策收集广告标识符、设备信息、IP 地址等。请参阅 Google 隐私政策 与 AdMob 说明。

本应用不要求注册账号，不收集姓名、邮箱、电话号码等账户信息（除非您主动通过邮件联系我们）。
